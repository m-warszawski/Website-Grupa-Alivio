RESPITE CARE



-> 24/7 CARE

Respite Centre of Grupa Alivio offers accommodation in a single or double, fully equipped room in a cozy, family place in the heart of Zielona Góra. Each room is equipped according to hotel standards. Guests have a choice between standard beds or rehabilitation beds with the possibility of position adjustment. A wireless paging system is installed next to each bed. Each room has a flat screen TV with a large selection of TV channels. WIFI is free in all areas. The Respite Centre is equipped with a monitoring system for safety of our clients.



-> DAY CENTRE

A day centre with comfortable armchairs, couches, a large TV with access to a wide range of TV channels, animation sets, books and magazines, as well as art materials is available for people staying with for whole day or just for a few hours.

We offer meals tailored to individual tastes and dietary requirements to all our guests.

Our Respite Centre also has a vehicle for wheelchair users, which our clients are very welcome to use..



BARRIER FREE ACCOMOADATION

As a part of the accommodation offer, we have single or double rooms with shared bathrooms in a building without architectural barriers. The package includes full board with snacks. Our guests have a choice between standard beds or rehabilitation beds with anti-bedsore mattresses. Each room is equipped with a wireless paging system. On special request, we offer a specialized hospital bed with a cellular mattress equipped with an air pump.

As part of the accommodation, our guests have access to medical equipment (hoist , toilet chair, transfer boards) and basic hygiene measures.

Our Respite Centre is located in the City Centre, has a private car park and free WIFI.

If necessary, our staff are always happy to help.

If you come to us with a assistant , he stays with us for FREE!



TRANSPORT FOR DISABLED

Grupa Alivio has a vehicle adapted for wheelchairs. As part of our services, we offer transport for the disabled people to any location. We can take you to the train station, airport or bring You for shopping. We will take You to the place on time and in nice company.
