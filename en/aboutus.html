ABOUT US

We are a group of people who have been dealing with disabled people for many years. Our passion enriched with numerous trainings and several years of professional experience in many European countries, gave us opportunity to create a friendly space for the elderly and disabled.

The Respite Centre operating as part of Grupa Alivio offers services for the elderly and disabled people when carers or Families are unable (due to fortuitous events or the need to rest) to take care of them . Our offer includes stationary hourly, daytime and 24-hour care. Regardless of the length of stay and range of care - professional, experienced and passionate staff, interior without architectural barriers, and rooms with modern equipment await for our clients.

Our headquarters is located in the city centre of Zielona Góra. In the immediate neighborhood is the Old Market Square and famous pedestrian zone of Zielona Góra. Within a few hundred meters there are, among others: University Hospital, City Hall, Marshal's Office, FOCUS PARK Shopping Centre, cinema, Theater, Museum, Concert Hall, as well as numerous restaurants and pubs.

Our centre has three fully equipped bedrooms, bathroom with shower, living room, office and small kitchen unit. All rooms are fully adapted for wheelchairs.
